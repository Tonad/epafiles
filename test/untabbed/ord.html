Research for the 21st Century

EPA is blazing a path forward to develop sustainable solutions to the nation's top environmental challenges.

The National Research Council published a report, Sustainability and the US EPA , which provides recommendations for achieving a sustainable future.

Working with partners and external stakeholders, EPA developed research frameworks in six priority areas that address the greatest needs of the nation for advancements in environmental research. The strategic research plans will be fully implemented in October 2011 after peer review.

Read the It All Starts with Science brochure (PDF) (2 pp, 815KB) to learn about EPA's six research priorities. Watch the It All Starts With Science video about how science protects us & our natural environment.
